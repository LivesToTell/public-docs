SMS Consent

Note: This page documents the SMS consent portion of the LivesToTell mobile app signup flow — screenshots are from the mobile app and not a webform.
Step 1
By default, the opt‑in checkbox is not selected and the "Send Code" button is disabled.
Step 2
When the user explicitly opts in during signup (before receiving any one‑time SMS verification codes), the "Send Code" button becomes enabled. Messages are transactional and used only for account security.
